CDG Gujarat, India

CDG is a supplying industry of roller bearings, ball bearings and automotive components with domestic manufacturing capabilities. Product offerings includes a full range of ball & roller bearings, mounted units, linear guides and ball screw support bearings. Bearing types include deep groove, angular contact, self-aligning, thrust and spherical. CDG also offers services including reconditioning, ball screw repair, replacement and reliable maintenance services.



Products

Linear Motion Bearings

This type of bearings are made up of Ball Screws, Linear Roller and Ball Guides, Support Units for Ball Screws, Mono carriers and Mega Torque Motors. Linear Motion Bearings generally use pad, bushing or roller system to carry a load on a rail that need not to be a straight line.

Automotive Components

Automotive Components such as Wheel Engine process, Transmissions, Various Accessories, Gearbox and Propellor Shafts are covered by bearings. A thrust bearing is a particular type of rotary rolling-element bearing which permits rotation between parts and support the axial load.

Machine Ball Bearings

Deep Groove Ball Bearings, SPACEA Ball Bearings, Angular Contact Ball Bearings, Super Precision Ball Bearings, Thrust Ball Bearings, Self-Aligning Ball Bearings.Ball Bearings uses balls to control separation between the bearing races. Also its purpose is to reduce friction to support radial loads.
